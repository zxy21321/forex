What is Myfxbook Autotrade?

The Myfxbook Autotrade application is a 3rd-party technology application made available to RT clients through the MT4 trading application.

Myfxbook Autotrade is an online automatic analytics tool that connects with your forex trading account. It allows users to:

  • Create an online portfolio to organize and monitor all trading accounts and systems in one centralised place.
  • Automatically analyse data in your trading system or account using the innovative statistical analysis tools.
  • Allow potential clients to see your trading performance and achievements by granting them access to see your 3rd party audited trading results.
  • Stay ahead of the curve with Myfxbook Autotrade’s economic events calendar,  market news, real-time forex quotes and other market indicators.

Facilitation of the Myfxbook Autotrade application is not to be construed as a recommendation to participate in any particular trading strategy or the use of the application by RT clients is appropriate or suitable for their circumstances. RT clients must make their own enquiries and determine whether use of the application is suitable for their needs.

 

Myfxbook aims to “to create a professional community for traders” whereby all user behaviour is transparent and audited. It aims to make the learning process simply, with assistance for both beginner and experienced traders.

Myfxbook Autotrade is a unique social and copy trading community application that promotes collaboration and sharing of ideas. It does this by connecting forex traders with their forex trading accounts. By connecting to Myfxbook, users can follow other traders or allow other traders to follow them.

  • See different trading strategies
    Follow traders with an established track record.
  • Share your trading strategy
    Myfxbook allows you to help others.
  • Diversify your risk by trading multiple strategies
    Diversify your trading by adding strategies to trade currencies, timeframes and models.
  • Monitor and track your trading performance
    Myfxbook automatically uploads your trading history from MT4 and provides you with detailed analysis on your performance free of charge. Without recording your results it’s difficult to know what to improve. Add this valuable service to your account today.
  • Analyze and improve your trading system
    Study your trading habits via the advanced statistical analysis and learn from the automatic analysis’ of your trading behaviour.
  • Store all of your trading systems in one place
    Organise and track all of your trading systems in one centralised portfolio.
  • Verify trading results
    Myfxbook Autotrade allows you to ask for an audit before you invest in system or provider, or let another trader trade with your money.
